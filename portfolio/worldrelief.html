Worldrelief.org

Platform: Blackbaud/Netcommunities

Global Intranet "The Hub"

Platform: SharePoint

Highlights: Built a complete intranet system start to finish. Created multiple custom web-apps to automate key workflows. Trained 8 administrators across the organization to make sure content and structure stayed relevant

Timeframe: 1 year

Project Phases

1. Design

Established core information architecture, taxonomy, policy and workflows as the foundational elements of the completed intranet.

2. Migrate

Established core information architecture, taxonomy, policy and workflows as the foundational elements of the completed intranet.

3. Train

Developed training experiences and worked with key leaders to drive adoption organization-wide, about 1,000 users across 14 countries.

4. Maintain

Worked closely with a "Hub Admin" in each department to identify needs and keep department sites current on a day-to-day basis. We automated some important document life-cycles to make sure content stays relevant without much human intervention.

5. Extend

Created custom apps for specific departments including Human Resources, Finance and International Programs. Finally, we integrated Yammer to provide a vibrant social component over time.

Overall Structure

With such a vast range of content, we knew people would struggle to find what they needed. So we established a comprehensive taxonomy and tagging system for all content, and allowed people to browse in two major ways: TYPE OF RESOURCE and DEPARTMENT OWNERSHIP. Combined with SEARCH, we tested the structure on new hires to get insight on how to make it better. We were able to modify the structure, taxonomy, and search function based on their feedback and experience. Our primary achievement was to establish an online platform that became the primary source for organizational knowledge across all departments and offices of World Relief.
